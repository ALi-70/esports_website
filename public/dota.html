Dota 2

Dota 2 is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where players control a powerful hero and battle against each other in fast-paced, strategic matches.

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is a popular multiplayer online battle royale game where players fight to be the last one standing on an ever-shrinking map.

Free Fire is a fast-paced, multiplayer online battle royale game where players fight to be the last one standing on a deserted island. With a variety of characters and weapons to choose from.
